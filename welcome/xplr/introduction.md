# Introduction

XPLR is the world's first comprehensive NFT trading platform based on algorithmic trading. Traditional NFT trading platforms such as OpenSea and LooksRare employ a single-point listing trading model, which is easily influenced by market sentiment and irrational investor behavior, leading to dramatic NFT price volatility and lack of liquidity during market downturns.

To address these challenges, XPLR has adopted an innovative **Algorithmic Trading Model (Pooling Algorithm Model)**. This model utilizes an automatic pricing system that ensures NFT prices automatically appreciate and maintains liquidity. This mechanism is not driven by market sentiment, providing liquidity to every NFT asset and protecting investors' rights and confidence.
