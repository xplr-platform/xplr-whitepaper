# Background

The NFT market has seen explosive growth, but it currently faces significant challenges:

1. **Lack of Liquidity**: Most NFTs are illiquid. Once the initial hype dies down, buyers cannot find sellers, and sellers cannot find buyers.
2. **Price Volatility**: Prices are highly susceptible to market sentiment and manipulation.
3. **High Entry Barriers**: High prices for premium NFTs prevent average users from participating.

XPLR was created to solve these exact problems through its algorithmic trading model, bringing stability, liquidity, and accessibility to the NFT market.
