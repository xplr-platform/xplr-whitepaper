# Referral Reward

Users can earn referral rewards from three generations of team members' transactions, including **Referral Trading Rewards** and **Referral Piggy Bank Rewards**.

| Level | Referral Reward | Referral Piggy Bank Reward | A-Level Referral | B-Level Referral | C-Level Referral | Piggy Bank Release Rate |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| LV2 | 0.90% | 0.40% | 0.55% | 0.25% | 0.10% | 20% |
| LV3 | 1.00% | 0.30% | 0.60% | 0.30% | 0.10% | 40% |
| LV4 | 1.10% | 0.20% | 0.65% | 0.30% | 0.15% | 60% |
| LV5 | 1.20% | 0.10% | 0.65% | 0.35% | 0.20% | 80% |
| LV6 | 1.30% | 0% | 0.70% | 0.40% | 0.20% | 100% |

**Piggy Bank Mechanism**: Referral rewards are deposited into the piggy bank according to individual level coefficients. When levels increase, the piggy bank reward coefficient decreases while the real-time trading reward coefficient increases. Each upgrade releases piggy bank balance proportionally. Validity period is 365 days.

**Reserve Jar**: Provides additional high-commission income for enthusiasts, requiring specific conditions (e.g., 1 year of participation, with 36 direct A-level referrals, 66 B-level referrals, and 66 C-level referrals).
